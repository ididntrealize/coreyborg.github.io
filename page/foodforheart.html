My contribution

My client wanted a beautiful and creative website that would be very cheap to maintain. She didn’t have a logo, slogan, or anything written up about her business. We worked together extensively to create purposeful content with a goal for conversion in mind. We wanted users to contact her about setting appointments.

I hosted the site on Github Pages for free, and I helped my client learn the in’s and out’s of the analytics I set up for her site.
